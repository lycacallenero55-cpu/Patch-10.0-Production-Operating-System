# Patch 10.0 Studio Constitution

## Purpose

This Constitution establishes the governing principles of Patch 10.0 Studio.

It defines the values, expectations, and organizational rules that apply to every department, production activity, repository asset, and contributor within the studio.

This document serves as the highest operational authority of the studio.

All governance documents, department manuals, workflows, and production standards shall conform to this Constitution.

---

# Scope

This Constitution applies to:

- Every department
- Every production cycle
- Every repository artifact
- Every work order
- Every assignment
- Every review
- Every production decision

No department is exempt.

---

# Constitutional Principles

## Principle 1 — The Studio Exists to Complete the Franchise

The primary objective of Patch 10.0 Studio is the successful completion and long-term maintenance of the Patch 10.0 franchise.

Individual deliverables shall always support the success of the franchise as a whole.

Departments shall optimize for long-term completion rather than isolated accomplishments.

---

## Principle 2 — The Repository Is the Single Source of Truth

The repository is the official record of the studio.

Repository content supersedes conversation history, temporary notes, and undocumented decisions.

Information that has not been committed to the repository shall not be considered official.

---

## Principle 3 — Clear Ownership

Every responsibility shall have exactly one owner.

Ownership includes:

- Decision authority
- Maintenance
- Quality
- Documentation
- Long-term stewardship

Departments may collaborate.

Ownership shall remain singular.

---

## Principle 4 — Respect Department Boundaries

Departments shall operate only within their assigned responsibilities.

Departments shall not assume ownership of another department's work unless explicitly authorized through governance or an approved work order.

Healthy collaboration shall never replace clear ownership.

---

## Principle 5 — Quality Before Quantity

The studio values durable quality over rapid production.

Departments shall not sacrifice consistency, maintainability, or canon integrity for short-term output.

---

## Principle 6 — Continuous Improvement

Every process, document, workflow, and standard may be improved when supported by evidence.

Improvement shall preserve or increase:

- Clarity
- Maintainability
- Scalability
- Production Quality

Unnecessary complexity shall be avoided.

---

## Principle 7 — Documentation Is Production

Documentation is not administrative overhead.

Documentation is a production asset.

Departments shall produce documentation that enables future work, preserves organizational knowledge, and reduces ambiguity.

Undocumented decisions should be treated as incomplete work.

---

## Principle 8 — Decisions Shall Be Justifiable

Significant decisions shall be supported by documented reasoning whenever practical.

Personal preference alone shall not justify permanent production decisions.

Where evidence exists, evidence shall take precedence over opinion.

---

## Principle 9 — Collaboration Over Isolation

The studio functions as a coordinated production organization.

Departments shall communicate through defined deliverables, documented interfaces, and approved workflows.

Success shall be measured by the effectiveness of the production pipeline rather than individual departments.

---

## Principle 10 — Protect the Canon

The integrity of the Patch 10.0 universe is a permanent responsibility.

No department shall knowingly introduce inconsistencies that compromise established canon.

Canon modifications shall follow the governance and review processes established by the studio.

---

## Principle 11 — Design for the Long Term

The studio is designed to support years of production.

Departments shall make decisions that reduce future maintenance costs whenever practical.

Temporary convenience shall not create permanent organizational debt.

---

## Principle 12 — The Studio Evolves

The studio is expected to evolve.

Departments, standards, workflows, templates, and documentation may change as production matures.

Evolution shall occur through deliberate improvement rather than uncontrolled growth.

---

# Organizational Expectations

All departments shall:

- Operate professionally.
- Respect ownership boundaries.
- Produce maintainable work.
- Preserve institutional knowledge.
- Support downstream departments.
- Accept constructive review.
- Improve continuously.
- Follow established governance.

---

# Authority

This Constitution has the highest authority within Patch 10.0 Studio.

If any governance document, department manual, template, workflow, or process conflicts with this Constitution, the Constitution shall prevail.

Conflicts shall be resolved through formal governance revisions rather than ad hoc exceptions.

# Closing Statement

Patch 10.0 Studio exists to transform ideas into a complete and enduring franchise through disciplined governance, specialized expertise, structured collaboration, and continuous improvement.

Every department serves the studio.

The studio serves the franchise.

The franchise serves its audience.
