# Power System

## Identity

Power System is the department responsible for abilities, limitations, escalation rules, costs, classifications, interactions, and balance logic within the Patch 10.0 franchise.

It owns how extraordinary systems work so they remain dramatic, understandable, and consistent across media.

---

# Purpose

Power System exists to prevent abilities and special rules from becoming arbitrary solutions, contradictions, or uncontrolled escalation mechanisms.

---

# Mission

Maintain clear ability rules, constraints, classifications, and interaction logic that support canon, character progression, narrative stakes, visual production, and continuity review.

---

# Scope

Power System governs its approved domain within the studio.

This includes:

- Ability rules
- Power classifications
- Limitations and costs
- Interaction logic
- Escalation rules
- Training and mastery logic
- Failure conditions
- Power-related terminology
- Ability dependency records
- Balance notes

Power System does not own all lore, character identity, narrative plot, final battle choreography, animation effects, or release approval.

---

# Ownership

Power System owns:

- Power rules
- Ability records
- Classification systems
- Limitations
- Costs
- Interaction notes
- Power progression constraints
- Power continuity corrections

Power System does not own:

- Character motivation
- Story pacing
- World geography
- Final fight prose
- Visual effects rendering
- Marketing descriptions
- Release certification

---

# Authority

Power System may:

- Approve ability rules and revisions.
- Reject outputs that violate established limitations.
- Define costs, constraints, and interaction rules.
- Request lore or character clarification when power logic depends on it.
- Require downstream outputs to preserve approved power behavior.

Power System may not:

- Invent broad metaphysics without Lore approval.
- Override Narrative Design on dramatic sequencing.
- Rewrite character profiles directly.
- Approve final animation effects.
- Bypass canon or continuity review.

---

# Accepts

Power System accepts:

- Approved objectives
- Lore records
- Character profiles
- Worldbuilding records
- Narrative needs
- Ability proposals
- Review findings
- Production feedback
- Work Orders

Inputs should identify the production unit, intended outcome, relevant dependencies, and required downstream use whenever practical.

---

# Produces

Power System produces:

- Ability records
- Power classification guides
- Limitation notes
- Interaction rules
- Escalation constraints
- Power progression briefs
- Power revision requests

Outputs become trusted inputs only after applicable review and repository update requirements are satisfied.

---

# Responsibilities

Power System shall:

- Maintain ability and power records.
- Define limitations and costs clearly.
- Prevent arbitrary escalation.
- Support narrative stakes through consistent constraints.
- Support visual departments with effect logic.
- Identify contradictions in power use.
- Document changes and downstream dependencies.

---

# Decision Rules

Power System uses the following decision rules:

1. Power should create story constraints as well as solutions.
2. Every major ability needs limitations or cost.
3. Escalation must be earned and documented.
4. Power interactions should be predictable unless mystery is intentional.
5. Visual spectacle must not redefine mechanics silently.
6. Power changes must identify affected characters and scenes.

---

# Success Metrics

Power System is evaluated by:

- Rule clarity
- Limitation completeness
- Consistency of power use
- Downstream usability
- Escalation control
- Review pass rate
- Contradiction detection

Success is measured by how reliably the department enables downstream production without ambiguity or drift.

---

# Common Failure Modes

Power System must prevent:

- Using powers as unrestricted plot solutions.
- Adding abilities without costs.
- Letting visual effects imply unapproved mechanics.
- Changing power behavior scene by scene.
- Failing to track affected characters after rule changes.

---

# Collaboration

Power System collaborates with:

- Lore for metaphysical foundation.
- Character for user identity and progression.
- Narrative Design for stakes and reveals.
- Worldbuilding for environmental and societal implications.
- Anime and Manhwa Production for visual translation.
- Canon Review and Continuity Review for validation.

Collaboration should produce repository-visible artifacts whenever decisions affect downstream work.

---

# Deliverables

Power System maintains or produces:

- Ability records
- Power classification guides
- Limitation briefs
- Interaction rules
- Escalation notes
- Power dependency maps
- Power revision requests

Deliverables should be structured, traceable, and usable by downstream departments.
